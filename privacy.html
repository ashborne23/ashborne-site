This Privacy Policy explains how Ashborne Partners collects, uses, and safeguards your personal information — whether you are visiting our website, exploring an opportunity with us, or engaging us to find leadership talent for your organisation. Your privacy matters to us, and we are committed to handling your information with care and transparency.

Ashborne Partners (“Ashborne,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is an executive search firm specialising in senior sales and revenue leadership placements for technology companies across North America. This policy applies to our Website Visitors, Candidates, Prospective Candidates, and Clients. We may update it from time to time; any changes will be posted on this page.

Who We Are

What Personal Data We Collect

Candidates

To help match you with the right leadership role, we collect information that is genuinely useful to that purpose, which may include:

  • Name and contact details
  • Employment history and current role
  • Education, qualifications, and professional certifications
  • Compensation expectations and current remuneration
  • References and referee details
  • Work authorisation status, where relevant to a role
  • Information about your career interests and the kind of opportunity you are seeking
  • Any additional information you choose to share with us

Prospective Candidates

Where we identify you through professional networks such as LinkedIn or other public professional sources, we may collect your employment history, qualifications, skills, and other details you have made publicly available, in order to assess whether we may be able to help you with a relevant opportunity.

Clients

We collect business contact details and role requirements for individuals at client organisations so that we can provide our search services and manage our engagement with you.

Website Visitors

We collect limited technical information about how our website is used — such as pages visited and general usage patterns — to help us improve the experience we offer.

How We Collect Your Data

We collect personal data directly from you (for example, when you send us your CV or contact us), from third-party professional sources (such as professional networking sites and public profiles), and automatically through your browser when you visit our website.

How We Use Your Data

Our primary purpose in using your personal data is to provide executive search services: to match candidates with suitable leadership roles and to introduce qualified candidates to our clients. We also use your information to communicate with you about relevant opportunities, to manage our client engagements, and to operate and improve our business. Where required by law, we will seek your consent before certain uses, such as marketing.

Who We Share Your Data With

We share personal data only where it supports the service we provide:

  • With prospective employers (our clients), to present you for relevant roles — always with appropriate care over what is shared
  • With trusted service providers who help us operate our business, under appropriate confidentiality obligations
  • Where required by law, regulation, or legal process

We do not sell your personal information.

How We Protect Your Data

We take reasonable and appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect personal data from misuse, loss, or unauthorised access. If you suspect any misuse or unauthorised access to your information, please contact us immediately at hello@ashbornepartners.co.in.

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide our services and to keep you informed of opportunities that may interest you. If you ask us to stop holding your data, we will delete it unless we are required to retain it for legal, tax, or regulatory reasons. In determining retention periods, we consider the nature and sensitivity of the data and the purposes for which we hold it.

Your Rights & Choices

You may contact us at any time to access, correct, update, or request deletion of the personal data we hold about you, or to opt out of marketing communications. To make a request, email hello@ashbornepartners.co.in. We may need to verify your identity before acting on your request, and we will respond in accordance with applicable law.

For California Residents

If you are a California resident, the California Consumer Privacy Act (as amended by the CPRA) gives you certain rights regarding your personal information. Within the preceding 12 months, we may have collected the following categories of personal information: identifiers (such as name and contact details); professional or employment-related information (such as work history, qualifications, and references); and inferences drawn from that information to assess suitability for roles.

We collect this information from the sources, and use it for the purposes, described elsewhere in this policy. We have not sold personal information as “sale” is defined under the CCPA.

California residents may request that we disclose the categories and specific pieces of personal information we have collected, the purposes for collecting it, and the categories of third parties with whom we have shared it; and may request that we delete personal information we collected from them. You have the right not to be discriminated against for exercising these rights. To make a request, contact us at hello@ashbornepartners.co.in.
