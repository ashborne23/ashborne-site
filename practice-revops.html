RevOps & Sales Operations

The operators who give revenue teams their systems, their data, and the forecasting discipline leadership relies on.

What the role really is

Revenue operations is the infrastructure beneath the number. A strong RevOps leader owns the systems, data, process, and analytics across the revenue funnel — the forecasting rigour, the tooling, the territory and comp design, and the single source of truth that lets leadership make decisions with confidence rather than instinct.

Why it is hard to get right

RevOps sits at the intersection of strategy, analytics, and technology, and few candidates are genuinely strong across all three. Many can run a CRM; far fewer can connect operational detail to revenue strategy and influence senior stakeholders. The mis-hire looks busy but never moves the metrics that matter.

How we approach it

We assess for the full triangle — strategic thinking, analytical depth, and systems fluency — and for the soft skill that makes RevOps effective: the ability to influence sales, marketing, and finance without owning them. We calibrate to the maturity of your existing operation.

  • VP / Head of Revenue Operations
  • Director of Sales Operations
  • RevOps & analytics leaders
  • Systems, forecasting & GTM data owners
